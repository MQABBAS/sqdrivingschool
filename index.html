Frequently Asked Questions

Does SQ Driving School cover both Birmingham and Manchester? ▾

Yes! SQ Driving School is a DVSA-approved driving school operating across both Birmingham (all B postcodes, Solihull, Sutton Coldfield) and Manchester (all M, SK, BL, OL, WN postcodes). Book in either city using the same number — 07352 932003.

How much do driving lessons cost? ▾

£33 per hour for both manual and automatic driving lessons — across both Birmingham and Manchester. A 10-hour block booking is £300. There is never a premium for automatic. No hidden fees, ever.

Is automatic really the same price as manual? ▾

Yes — always. Both manual and automatic are £33/hr at SQ Driving School. We genuinely never charge a premium for automatic. One of the very few driving schools in Birmingham and Manchester that truly means it.

What is your first-time pass rate? ▾

SQ Driving School achieves a 90%+ first-time pass rate — nearly double the UK national average of approximately 46%. We achieve this through structured lessons, realistic mock tests, and expert knowledge of all Birmingham and Manchester test routes.

Do you offer intensive driving courses? ▾

Yes! Intensive courses from 10 to 40 hours, starting from £300. Available across Birmingham and Manchester. Most learners are test-ready in 2–4 weeks. Manual or automatic — same price. WhatsApp us to check current availability.

How do I book — is WhatsApp fastest? ▾

Yes! WhatsApp 07352 932003 is the fastest way — we reply within minutes. You can also use the booking form on this page or call us directly. We respond to all enquiries within 2 hours.

I'm very nervous — is that okay? ▾

Absolutely. Many of our most successful learners started very anxious. Our instructors are calm, patient, and go entirely at your pace — in both Birmingham and Manchester. No pressure, no rushing, no judgment.
