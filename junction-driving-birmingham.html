Driving at Junctions — Birmingham Lessons Guide

Junctions are the most common location for driving test faults in Birmingham. The city's complex mix of T-junctions, crossroads, staggered junctions, and box junctions demands precise observation, correct positioning, and confident decisions.

Types of Junctions in Birmingham Tests

  • T-junctions — emerge left or right; give way to all traffic on the major road
  • Crossroads — position according to your intended turn; yield correctly based on road markings
  • Box junctions — only enter if your exit is clear; stop on yellow only when turning right
  • Traffic light junctions — common on Birmingham ring roads; position early in the correct lane

Effective Observation at Junctions

The MSPSL routine (Mirror, Signal, Position, Speed, Look) must be completed smoothly at every junction. Emerging without adequate observation is the most common cause of serious test faults in Birmingham.
